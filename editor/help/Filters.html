Filters

Filters - a way to identify physicals by their category.
  • detector - invisible objects, serving as... detectors (whatever it means)
  • player
  • monster
  • bullet
  • lair
  • wall
  • item
  • door
  • trap - not used
  • region - see: regions
  • obstacle - barrels etc.

    • Additional:
  • hostiles - monster, lair
  • friendly - player
  • actors - hostiles, friendly
  • static - bullet, wall, item, door
  • moving - detector, player, monster, bullet
  • nonmoving - lair, wall, item, door
  • pathblocker - wall, door
  • destructible - actors, obstacle
